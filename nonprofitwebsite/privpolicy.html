Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how the Rindler Institute of Quantitative Education (“RIQE”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use this website or interact with our program.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Contact information (name, email, etc.)
  • Application information (academic details, links, experience)
  • Technical information such as basic analytics or hosting logs

2. How We Use Information

  • Review and evaluate applications
  • Communicate with applicants and participants
  • Improve our website and program
  • Generate non-identifying statistics

3. Information Sharing

  • With organizers or reviewers helping run RIQE
  • With service providers (hosting, email, etc.)
  • When required by law

4. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as needed for program-related purposes or legal obligations. RIQE is intended for applicants who are at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. We do not sell your personal information. We take reasonable measures to protect application data, but no system is perfectly secure. In general, we retain application records as we see fit for program tracking and alumni outreach, unless you ask us to delete them sooner where legally permitted.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We may use simple analytics tools or hosting logs. If this changes, this policy will be updated.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data where applicable.

7. Contact
