Invest in Quantitative Education

Your support helps RIQE provide research-style experiences, mentorship, and scholarship-style support to driven students exploring quantitative finance, mathematics, and computation.

Donations help cover data access, compute resources, curriculum development, guest speakers, and need-based support for participants who might not otherwise be able to take part.

Status & Transparency

RIQE is not currently a tax-exempt entity. If you choose to contribute, your payment goes to a personal account until a RIQE bank account is active. My intention is to use these funds to support early RIQE expenses (data, compute, curriculum work, etc.) while I work toward formally establishing the nonprofit.
